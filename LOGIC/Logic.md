
Main Logic File — Interactive Investor Presentation Portal

Project Purpose

Build a premium, black, luxury-style investor presentation portal for a high-level capital raise centered on an integrated platform spanning Home Services, Solar and Real Estate, and Aerial Insights. The final product must function as a self-guided investor briefing environment that communicates the opportunity with authority, clarity, and excitement, while also allowing sophisticated viewers to interact with assumptions and understand the financial logic beneath the projections.

This is not a generic website and not a simple pitch deck conversion. It is a private capital presentation interface designed to:

present the overall opportunity at a high level
explain each operating division in depth
visualize financial growth and capital deployment
allow the presenter or viewer to adjust major business levers in real time
update charts and outputs dynamically based on those changes
explain every meaningful number through tooltips, drawers, modals, and assumption references
feel luxurious, modern, serious, cinematic, and investor-grade
The final experience must be strong enough that an investor can review the portal without a live presenter and still come away understanding the business, the model, the strategic logic, and the upside.

Core Experience Principles

1. Premium investor experience
The interface must feel exclusive and high-caliber. The user should feel like they are entering a private digital deal room, not a startup landing page.

2. Layered information architecture
The experience must never overwhelm the user. Information should unfold in layers:

layer 1: executive summary and strategic overview
layer 2: division-level breakdowns
layer 3: financial projections and charts
layer 4: adjustable assumptions
layer 5: formulas, definitions, notes, and investor clarifications
3. Visual first, numbers always central
The presentation should be highly visual, cinematic, and engaging, but the numbers and projections remain the center of gravity. Every visual exists to support credibility, understanding, or emotional impact.

4. Controlled interactivity
The portal should allow users to adjust meaningful variables that materially influence performance outcomes. Interactivity must feel smooth and premium, not gimmicky.

5. Explanation for every meaningful number
Every key metric, output, and chart must be explainable through an info system. No large number should appear without an accessible explanation of what it represents and what drives it.
